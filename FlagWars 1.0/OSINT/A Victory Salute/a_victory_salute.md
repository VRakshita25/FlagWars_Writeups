# A Victory Salute (150 points)

## Description:
Under the floodlights, a star raises his hand — a fleeting gesture capturing triumph, focus, and the roar of thousands. Every banner, color, and shadow in the stadium tells a story. Can you uncover who he is and where this moment took place?

## Solution:
Upon doing a reverse image search of the given image in google lens, it is revealed that the person in the picture is Jos Buttler. This picture is from an IPL 2025 match between GT and DC were Buttler scored 97. This match had taken place in the home ground of the Gujarat Titans, the Narendra Modi Stadium.
