On the first page, written in purple ink, is a riddle:

“The candy that never melts hides the key to my sweetest secret.
Only those who can unwrap the old seal shall taste the forbidden formula.”

Rumor has it that Willy Wonka left behind one final recipe "The Everlasting Gobstopper 2.0", a candy that can replicate infinite flavors from memory. But to prevent it from falling into the wrong hands, he encrypted it twice once using his old-school cipher machine, and again using modern magic (RSA) developed by his new apprentice, Charlie Bucket.

Now, you the newest intruder in the chocolate factory must decipher the vault and uncover the lost formula.
