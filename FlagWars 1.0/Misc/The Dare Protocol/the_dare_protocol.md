# The Dare Protocol (250 points)

## Description:
Not all flags are found in code. Some are earned through courage.

Before you lies a set of encrypted paper tokens — chits, each containing a unique “social exploit.” Choose one and execute it. The task may challenge your comfort zone, but completion will yield the flag you seek.

In this game, your exploit isn’t digital — it’s personal.

## Solution:
Pick a chit from the lot and perform a dare. Upon successfully completing the dare, you are presented with the flag.

## Flag:
flagwars{courage_unlocked}
